We combine **massive human factors data with large model technologies** to build computational models that simulate, predict, and optimize user experience.

### Key Topics

- **AI-powered usability evaluation** — intelligently assessing design usability at early stages of product development
- **User behavior prediction** — predicting user reactions in dangerous or critical events
- **LLM-driven design tools** — leveraging large language models to empower design innovation and creativity
- **Multimodal experience modeling** — integrating behavioral, physiological, and contextual data for holistic UX understanding

### Representative Work

- *SimUser* — generating usability feedback by simulating various users interacting with mobile applications (CHI, 2024)
- *SmartPaint* — a co-creative drawing system based on generative adversarial networks (FITEE, 2019)
- *Interesting and impressive* — exploring design factors for product GIFs to enhance engagement (Design Science, 2023)
